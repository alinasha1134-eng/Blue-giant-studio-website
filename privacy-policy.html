Privacy Policy

Learn how we process, handle and encrypt applicant and client variables.

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

1. Information Collection Practices

At Blue Giant Studio, we collect essential details submitted directly via our package submission and contact forms. This includes name, company details, email addresses, and WhatsApp phone variables. We also capture anonymous analytics metadata (IP address, browser parameters, cookies) to improve frontend operations.

2. How We Utilize Your Data

The collected variables are processed exclusively for:

  • Compiling custom digital marketing proposal briefs.
  • Direct WhatsApp/Email communication regarding inquiries.
  • Improving search attribution and Lighthouse speed metrics.
  • Providing client access channels to analytics dashboards.

3. NDA & Information Protection

All shared marketing data, product niches, and ad account statistics are classified as strictly confidential. We deploy encrypted transmission parameters and do not share, sell, or rent business variables to third-party databases.

4. Client Control Options

You hold absolute control over your records. You can request the direct deletion of your lead parameters or contact information from our database at any time by contacting our support team at info@bluegiantstudio.com.
